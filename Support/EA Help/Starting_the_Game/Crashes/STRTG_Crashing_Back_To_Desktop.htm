Crashing Back To Desktop

If your game is crashing back to the Windows desktop before play begins follow the guide below that best describes the situation.

Copy Protection Crash:  The game crashes before even entering a full screen mode.  A title screen is shown for a moment and then the game disappears.
Crash After Full Screen:  The game closes after entering a full screen mode (often from a full black or white screen).  

For all other types of crashes check our Tech Support web site for known issues at http://techsupport.ea.com

 

 

 

EA Tech Tip:

A large majority of crashes are related to problems communicating with a computer's video card.
